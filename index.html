Our Story

Sometimes the most beautiful love stories begin in the most ordinary places.

Our story began in June 2023 as colleagues at work. Neither of us knew that the person we would one day call home was already standing quietly in the background of our lives. What started as simple conversations gradually became friendship, and friendship slowly blossomed into something neither of us could ignore.

The more time we spent together, the more we discovered in each other — someone who shared our values, our faith, our dreams, and our sense of humour.

In November 2023, we began our journey as boyfriend and girlfriend. Through every season, we remained committed to choosing each other, building a relationship founded on love, trust, and faith in God.

In March 2026, at London's most iconic restaurant — The Aqua Shard — Robertson asked Oluwaseun to spend forever with him. It was the easiest "yes" and one of the happiest moments of our lives.

"He has made everything beautiful in its time." — Ecclesiastes 3:11

What began as two separate journeys has become one shared story centred on Christ. We look forward to growing together, laughing together, travelling together, and building a life that honours God in every season.

Travel & Stay

We are delighted that you will be joining us in Glasgow to celebrate our special day. Here is some useful travel and accommodation information.

✈️ By Air

  • Glasgow Airport (GLA) — approx. 20 minutes from the city centre
  • Edinburgh Airport (EDI) — approx. 1 hour from Glasgow by car, bus, or train

🚆 By Train

Glasgow is well connected by rail from across the UK. Main stations include:

  • Glasgow Central Station
  • Glasgow Queen Street Station

Both are located in the city centre, a short distance from the reception venue.

🚖 Getting Around

Uber, local taxis and buses are widely available throughout Glasgow.

The reception venue is approximately 10 minutes by car and 13 minutes by bus from the cathedral.

🏨 Accommodation

We recommend staying in Glasgow city centre or nearby areas such as Paisley for convenient access to both venues.

  • Maldron Hotel Glasgow City
  • Clayton Hotel Glasgow
  • Motel One Glasgow
  • Leonardo Royal Hotel Glasgow
  • Holiday Inn Glasgow Airport
  • Normandy Hotel

⚠️ Accommodation Notice

Our wedding takes place during the final weekend of the Glasgow 2026 Commonwealth Games, which means accommodation may be busier and more expensive than usual. We strongly recommend booking as early as possible.

Guests may also consider staying in nearby Paisley, Stirling, or Edinburgh, which offer excellent transport links. Edinburgh is approximately 50 minutes from Glasgow by train.

If you need any assistance, please feel free to contact us.
